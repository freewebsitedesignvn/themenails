Welcome to Bollinger Nail Salon!!!

About Us
Opened since 1998 with a single location in San Ramon, California. Bollinger Nail Salon was established with the vision of providing superior service to our customers in a relaxing, upscale environment.

Currently, we have six salons located in San Ramon, Pleasant Hill, Walnut Creek, Pleasanton, Concord and Danville.
What you can come to expect…
A salon where you receive the finest nail care in a comfortable and inviting atmosphere. We strive to assure that our customers receive the very best in personalized and professional nail care services.

Our friendly, licensed professionals provide a wide selection of nail enhancements in a carefully maintained hygienic environment. We also offer complete manicure and pedicure services.
