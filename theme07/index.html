About Us
At Elite Nail Spa, we offer top quality nail care services using high-end products from OPI, Creative Cuccio, Keyano, and Qtica. ELITE Nail Spa prides itself for staying completely up to date on the latest trends, treatments and safety in the nail industry. We Strive to be knowledge on your nail health so that we can work together to keep your hands and feet healthy.

ELITE Nail Spa has made a commitment to provide the cleanest, safest nail experience in the Cobb area. We want to communicate with you about your needs, concerns, and suggestions. It is our goal to make ELTIE Nail Spa a cozy, personal nail salon, with individual attention to each client.
