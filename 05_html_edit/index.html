Our Story
Our Team of Certified Designers are here to offer you all the latest trends with the best quality products. We specialize in all areas of hair design, hair coloring both new & corrective, bridal services, hair extensions, integration systems, permanent makeup, skin care, nail care.

We have several convenient locations in West Palm Beach and Broward county. Boynton Beach, Jupiter, Deerfield Beach, and Fort Lauderdale are just to name a few. At our salon your happiness is our goal, and our salon staff is responsible for providing outstanding service. We always offer free consultations. Our clients particularly value our salon and we look forward to meeting you soon!
Philosophy
At Tipsy Salon Bar we nurture a deep understanding and passion for wellness, which we define as a way of life with a focus on natural emotional and mental well being. We are committed to delivering all aspects of wellness with integrity and honesty, providing inspiration for lifestyle transformation, in an environment that delights your body, mind and spirit. Using exclusive organic nail and spa products has enabled us to create a distinctive spa, a concept with a unique experience for the senses in the heart of the city of Vienna, VA.
Our Spa Experience
Nurturing deep respect, understanding and care for the uniqueness of each individual are the key elements comprising the essence of our spa experience. Our treatments combine both ancient and contemporary techniques in a natural manner using organic and eco-friendly products. The natural products we employ are uniquely qualified to restore balance and equilibrium, while meeting the personally tailored needs of each individual.
